Deliveroo Customers To Get Exclusive Dibs On Burger King’s Double Ultimate Rendang Angus Beef Burger This National Day!

  • 7 July 2021
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 4 min
national day burger king rendang angus beef burger
Share this article
  • Available from 9 to 12 July, be kiasu, and proud as you chow down on this patriotic feast!
  • From 13 July, also look forward to the return of the Classic Double Rendang Beef and Rendang Tendergrill® Chicken Burgers
  • Plus, keep your eyes peeled for a limited-edition Deliveroo and BURGER KING® National Day keepsake to be announced later this month

What's the one thing that brings Singaporeans together? Our love for food! This National Day, fast food fans can rejoice as Deliveroo and BURGER KING® come together for the third year to celebrate our nation's 56th birthday with a limited level-up-timed launch, exclusively available on Deliveroo for home delivery from 9 to 12 July.  Just in time for an at-home celebration with loved ones!

During this period, Deliveroo customers will be the first in the Little Red Dot to get their hands on the new Double Ultimate Rendang Angus Beef Burger and the Rendang Shiok Shake Up, all delivered straight to customers' doorsteps via Singapore's favourite food delivery platform.

A level-up from the much-loved Rendang Burger OG, the Double Ultimate Rendang Angus Beef Burger ($12.50 a la carte) features two juicy, tender Angus flame-grilled beef patties, topped with BURGER KING®'s signature, sedap Rendang sauce and fresh onions, and for the first time this year, strips of crispy turkey bacon will be nestled between fluffy corn-dusted sourdough buns.

The first 560 die-hard fans who opt to upsize their Double Ultimate Rendang Angus Beef Burger meal via Deliveroo, priced at $14.60, between 9 to 12 July will be handsomely rewarded with a Rendang Shiok Shake Up (U.P. $5.20), a cup size serving of up to 6 nuggets with fries soaked in even more Rendang sauce, completely on the house.

But that's not all! From 13 July onwards, Deliveroo customers can continue to order-in their new favourite indulgence, the Double Ultimate Rendang Angus Beef Burger, and also look forward to the return of the classic Double Rendang Beef and Rendang Tendergrill® Chicken Burgers, both priced at $7.50 a la carte, plus larger bundle sets to feed the entire family. From 30 July onwards, the Taste of Singapore Indulgence meal will also be exclusive to Deliveroo customers, perfect for those looking for a hearty mid-week treat, and priced at a pocket-friendly $15.

Pssst, want to profess your love for Rendang by wearing your heart on your sleeve? Watch this space for a limited-edition Deliveroo and Burger King National Day collectible coming your way later this month. Shiok!
