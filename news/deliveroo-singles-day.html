Treat yourself this Singles’ Day with Deliveroo’s delicious $11 deals

  • 5 November 2020
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 2 min
singles' day
Share this article
  • With Deliveroo, there are more reasons to relish singledom!

This November, to celebrate singlehood and the joys that come with flying solo (including not having to share your food), Singapore's most loved food delivery service, Deliveroo, has teamed up with a range of restaurants to offer a delectable array of fan favourite meals at a delicious price of $11.

From 9 to 15 November, treat your body like a temple with a Samba set from SMOOSHA by SaladStop! that packs a nutritious juice shot to boost your immunity and a supercilious acai bowl. Feeling a little more adventurous? Travelling may not be possible right now, but you can take your tastebuds around the world with Deliveroo. Take your pick of $11 pasta dishes from La Mensa - from a hearty bowl of carbonara to a satisfying bowl of Aglio Olio. For a good ol' Mexican feast for one, check out Muchachos and get your fix of burrito, burrito bowl, quesadilla or salad bowl at just, you guessed it, $11 each! From sweet to savoury, there is surely something for you this Singles' Day.
