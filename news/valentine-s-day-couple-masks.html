Steal A Pizza My Heart This Valentine’s Day With Limited-Edition Deliveroo and Pizza Hut Couples Face Masks

  • 1 February 2021
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 3 min
valentine's day couple masks
Share this article
  • A pizza night in and a thoughtful gift? It's the only way to say I love a pizza you!

The most romantic month of the year is upon us, which means loved up couples and feasting aplenty! If you're coupled with your favourite foodie on February 14, what better way to celebrate Valentine's Day in the new norm than with a pair of limited-edition his and hers face masks by Deliveroo, created in partnership with fast-food favourite Pizza Hut Exclusively available with pre-orders of the Valentine's Day Combo from participating Pizza Hut restaurants islandwide, it's love at first slice.

With face masks now the hottest (and safest) fashion accessory of the season, couples can now proudly wear their hearts on their masks this Valentine's. Designed by local illustrator Tiffany Tan (@lovage) and boutique face mask store (@laylaluna.co), the handmade fabric masks are artfully designed with an assortment of cute and quirky illustrations and puns, complete with Deliveroo and Pizza Hut-styled graphics. Each pair comes in two eye-catching colours --  purple and teal, and kelp and teal; perfect to don when you're out and about with bae!

If you're planning on beating the crowds and staying-in to enjoy an intimate dinner for two this Valentine's, Pizza Hut is offering a whopping 60% off their limited-edition Valentine's Day Combo. At only $26.60 SGD (RRP $66.508.80) it's a mouthwatering steal you won't want to miss. The special promotion deal is available for pre-orders on 13 February and pick-up/delivery on 14 February at selected Pizza Hut outlets on Deliveroo*. Mark your calendars now as there's only a limited number of sets up for grabs!

Suitable for two to three pax, each Deliveroo Valentine's Day Pizza Hut Combo  consists of:

  • 1 pair of Deliveroo x Pizza Hut custom-designed face masks
  • 1 Large Pizza (Classic)
  • 1 Pasta & Baked Rice (choice of Turkey Bacon or Prawn Aglio Olio/Curry Zazzle Baked Rice
  • 1 choice of Appetiser (choice of 6pcs Sweet N Spicy Drumlets or 6pcs Honey Roasted Wing)
  • 4pcs Chocolate Hazelnut Tart

What's more, Deliveroo customers can now leave their riders a token of their appreciation thanks to Deliveroo's new post-order tipping function, by nominating an amount upon completion of their delivery, the entirety of which will go directly to the rider. To encourage and motivate customers to show some love to their riders this February, from 1 to 14 of the month, Deliveroo will match customer tips up to the value of $3 max per tip, capped at $10,000.
