Deliveroo Unveils Fantastic Feasts and Where to Find Them

  • 12 November 2018
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 4 min
fantasticfeast harryporter map foodrecommendations
Share this article
  • Order-in inspiration for your next magical meal

In celebration of this year's most anticipated movie sequel, Deliveroo is asking Singapore to get in the wizarding spirit as it unveils "Fantastic Feasts and Where to Find Them." From North to South, East to West, Singapore's most-loved food delivery service has compiled a guide to the Little Red Dot's most magical menus. Trust us when we say that no stone has been left unturned for us mere muggles!

So where can the most fantastic feasts on the island be found?

West 

●        'Scrumptious' Sushi – Sushi Tei, Jurong East – First up in the 'Weird and Wonderful' West, get your chopsticks around Sushi Tei's scrumptious sushi. From Special Rolls to Tempura, Sushi Tei always makes for a rollin' good time!

●        'Kampong-Style' Fare – New Ubin Seafood, Upper Bukit Timah – Next up is New Ubin Seafood's kampong-style fare. Traditional Satay, Hokkien Mee and "Cze Char" Fried Rice are the order of the day at this truly Singaporean experience.

●        'Sensational' Salads – The Daily Cut, Buona Vista – The health conscious amongst us can get their five-a-day fix with The Daily Cut. Pick from an endless array of bases, proteins and supplements, finished with tantalising toppings and delicious dressings, for a truly sensational salad.

●        'Perfect' Peranakan, Ivin's Peranakan Restaurant, Binjai Park – Look no further than Ivin's Restaurant for Udang Sembal, Nasi Goreng and Beef Rendang, just perfect Peranakan guaranteed every time.

East

●        'Made-From-Scratch' Indian Curries – Indian Curry House, Upper East Coast – Over in the East, Butter Chicken, Mama Mia Mutton and Paneer Makhani are made-from-scratch and available at the touch of a button (or a wave of a wand) from Indian Curry House.

●        'Bubblicious' Bubble Tea – Gong Cha, Eunos – For those with a sweet tooth in Eunos, slurp away on Pearl Milk, Brown Sugar, and Honey Lemon Green Tea from our bubbilicious favourite Gong Cha – where the Bubble Tea magic happens!

●        Favourite Fire-Baked Pizza, Alt. Pizza, Katong – Katong residents can have a pizza party seven days a week with Alt. Pizza's favourite fire-baked pizzas. Tuck into a Truffle Shuffle, Hawaiian Hippie or Fowl Play for a flavorsome carb fix.

CBD 

●        'Like-Your-Mum-Makes' Porridge, Ah Chiang's Porridge, Toa Payoh – For Porridge just like your mum make it, Ah Chiang serves up Fish, Pork and Cuttlefish variations to warm the souls (and stomachs) of Toa Payoh's hungry customers.

●        'Melt-In-Your-Mouth' Pastries, Tiong Bahru Bakery, Tiong Bahru – Those residing in the hipster hood of Tiong Bahru can enjoy melt-in-the-mouth pastries aplenty. Whether it's TBB signature croissant, the Kouign Amann, or Roasted Vegetable Quiche, these sweet and savoury treats are worth every single calorie.

●        'Gourmet' Burgers, Three Buns Potato Head, Keong Saik Road – The CBD have the pick of the gourmet burger bunch with Three Buns Potato Head. Black Angus beef patties, buttermilk fried chicken or Irish brown crab sandwiched between toasted brioche, rice or wholemeal buns will curb carnivorous cravings.

●        Authentic Greek Cuisine, Blu Kouzina, Dempsey – No need to travel over 5,000 miles to Greece, when Dempsey dwellers can enjoy Blu Kouzina's authentic greek cuisine delivered straight to their door. Must-try Pita Wraps, Hot Mezes and Skewers are all worth smashing some plates for!

North 

●        'Smashing' Ayam Penyet, Ayam Penyet President, Yishun – Northerners can enjoy smashing fried chicken amongst other Indonesian delicacies from Ayam Penyet. Sides of Gado-Gado and Chicken Satay will make this a meal to remember!

●        'Local-Favourite' Chicken Rice, Boon Tong Kee, Ang Mo Kio – Last but certainly not least, Boon Tong Kee serves up our local favourite Chicken Rice to those craving some of best Singapore has to offer.

So what are you waiting for? Wizard or non-wizard, all of Deliveroo's Fantastic Feasts are available straight to your door year-round. Now go forth and feast!
