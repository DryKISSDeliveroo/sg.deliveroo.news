This Valentine’s Day, Say ILY with Deliveroo and KFC’s Secret Recipe to My Heart

  • 11 February 2019
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 3 min
valentinesday
Share this article
  • Because food is the mother-clucking way to bae's heart

Roses are red, violets are blue, flowers will fade, how 'bout fried chicken for two? This year, Deliveroo has teamed up with Kentucky Fried Chicken® (KFC) to create a 'Secret Recipe to My Heart' combo meal to spice up your Valentine's Day. Because nothing says 'I Love You' like fried chicken, come February 14th, why not skip the roses or chocolates and treat your loved one to a finger-lickin' good meal instead? The best part: we'll deliver straight to your doorstep, so no queues, no crowds, no fuss, just you, your favourite person and darn good fried chicken.

The 'Secret Recipe to My Heart' meal, perfect for a cosy dine-in dinner for two, includes five pieces of Original Recipe or Hot & Crispy Chicken, 3 pieces Hot & Crispy Tenders, choice of 2 sides (Cheese Fries, large Fries or medium Whipped Potato) and choice of 2 drinks (Sjora Mango Peach, Iced Milo, Lipton Iced Tea or Pepsi), all from a cool $25.95. Who said you need a fancy restaurant to impress?

The winner, winner, (date-night) chicken dinner comes in an oh-so-Instagrammable heart shaped box. For extra brownie points with boo, you can even be like Shakespeare and personalise your box with a heartfelt message. When prompted at checkout, simply pen your words of affection at the "Allergy Note" box before completing your order, and on arrival, watch as your other half is blown away by your thoughtful sweet nothings.

The 'Secret Recipe to My Heart' meal in limited-edition personalisable box is available exclusively via Deliveroo for pre-order from Wednesday, 13 February, for delivery this Valentine's Day, Thursday 14 February, from 5 KFC restaurants - Chinatown Point, Clementi Mall, Far East Plaza, Parkway Parade and Serangoon Central.* Customers can search for restaurants under Western, Chicken and Fried Chicken tags. It'll simply be love at first bite!

Even if you're not having date night, you can still get a piece of the action with over 50 KFC branches now serving up their crispy, juicy and succulent delights on Deliveroo, customers islandwide can delight in the convenience of having KFC at just a click away, with the Deliveroo app. Whether for a solo dinner or a raucous party of ten, everyone can appreciate having a 'Two Pieces Chicken Meal' or 'Fifteen Pieces Chicken Feast' delivered piping hot to their doorstep. From hot deals to buddy meals, buckets to feasts, there's something for every chicken craving!

*While stocks last at all branches
