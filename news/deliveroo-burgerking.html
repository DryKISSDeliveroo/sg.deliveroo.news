Deliveroo Customers First to Get Their Hands on Burger King’s Popular Plant-based WHOPPER® in Singapore on Special 1-for-1 Promotion

  • 17 June 2021
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 3 min
deliveroo burger king plant based whopper
Share this article
  • Indulge your fast food cravings while chowing down consciously!

As the plant-based phenomenon continues to sweep Singapore, Burger King fans can rejoice as the fast food favourite finally launches its much-loved Plant-based WHOPPER® in the Little Red Dot. Available from tomorrow, 18 June, Deliveroo customers will be the first to get their hands-on the Plant-based WHOPPER® via Singapore's favourite food delivery platform, Deliveroo. There's never been a better time to kick back, relax and give yourself a night off from the kitchen with the newest plant-based burger in town – all while being kind to the environment.

100% flame grilled, but with 0% beef, the Plant-based WHOPPER® features a delicious plant-based patty from The Vegetarian Butcher, served between toasted sesame buns, and topped with freshly sliced tomatoes, fresh lettuce, creamy mayonnaise, ketchup, crunchy pickles, and sliced white onions. Made by meat lovers for meat lovers, the burger is priced at just $7.90 a la carte, or hungry customers can also make the burger a meal with Deliveroo and Burger King's limited time combo deal, priced at just $10 SGD for a pocket-friendly 1-for-1 Plant-based WHOPPER®, fries and Coca-cola Zero. There's only 500 1-for-1 combo deals available so it'll be the fastest fingers first to get a taste of this plant-based deliciousness on launch day!

All about being better for the planet and kinder to animals, The Vegetarian Butcher is on a mission to become the largest butcher in the world with meat without animals. With a 'no meat substitutes, only meat successors,' ethos, the launch of Burger King's Plant-based WHOPPER® in Singapore makes it even easier to make sustainable food choices thanks to its iconic flame-grilled taste

The Plant-based WHOPPER® a la carte and $10 1-for-1 combo meal will both be available exclusively for home delivery from participating Burger King restaurants* via Deliveroo from 18-21 June. New Deliveroo customers can also enjoy a further $10 off $20 spend on all Burger King orders with the code PLANTWHOPPER, valid till 30 Sept 2021. Long live the King!

*For a full list of participating Burger King outlets via Deliveroo, refer to the appendix below. **$10 1-for-1 promotion is capped at 500 sets,only available on the Plant-based WHOPPER combo meal and not on the a la carte burger.
