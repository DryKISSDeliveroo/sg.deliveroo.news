Give your health a boost while working from home with Deliveroo

  • Read time: 5 min

  • Who said lockdown lunches can't be healthy?

Down to the last two weeks of circuit breaker, you've likely established new #stayhome routines that may include productivity hacks, virtual workouts or Houseparty hangouts with family and friends. But if you're finding yourself making your fourth trip to the refrigerator and it's only 11 am, it's time to fix that boredom eating and switch to healthier habits instead. Singapore's most-loved food delivery service, Deliveroo, has you covered with a host of nutritious options from your favourite restaurants, delivered right to your doorstep.

Eating healthy, regular meals is a good way to keep mindless snacking in check. We've put together a go-to list of healthy dishes that you can count on to sate your hunger pangs, give your dose of nutrition and keep your wallet happy with select promotions. Get inspired from the menu of must-try dishes, perfect for a light snack, a feast for the family or a simple and hearty meal for yourself!
