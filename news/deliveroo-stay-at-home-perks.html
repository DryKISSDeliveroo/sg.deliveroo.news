Deliveroo Launches Stay-At-Home Perks To Up Your Dine-in Game

  • 1 April 2020
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 6 min
deliveroo stay-at-home perks
Share this article
  • Continue to support your favourite restaurants from the comfort of your home

As Singapore continues to do its part to stop the spread of COVID-19 together, the nation's most loved food delivery service, Deliveroo, is now bringing stay home perks so customers can continue to enjoy restaurant meals in the comfort of their home. Ideal for those currently working from home, having a chillaxing weekend indoors, or just those looking to take a break from kitchen meal prep, there's a restaurant and promotion to suit even the fussiest of foodies.

As more Singaporeans stay home to flatten the curve, Deliveroo has upped the number of new restaurants and promotions to cater to more customers staying in. From large chains to local family favourites to quick takeaways, over 600 new restaurants like MOS Burger, Food Republic, Isaac Toast and Mellben Signature & Seafood have joined the platform since late January. Hungry customers can simply search "stay home" on the Deliveroo app to browse all available promotions in their area, and use code STAYHOME15 or STAYHOME30 to enjoy free delivery which is applicable across over 100 participating outlets including Soup Restaurant and Fatboy's The Burger Bar with a minimum spend of $15 or $30 respectively, with discount capped at $3. Other key offers include:

  • Subway From now until 12 April, enjoy free delivery with min $16 spend using code SUBWAYFD (limited redemptions).
  • Collin's Common Grill From now until 31 April, enjoy 50% off Combo Grill and bundles including a mushroom soup (original price $29).
  • Tapas Club From now until 5 April, enjoy 40% off 10 best selling items including Mushroom Croquettes, Squid in its own ink, and more.
  • A-One Claypot House From now until 30 April, enjoy 1-for-1 Gong Bao Chicken Bento Set (original price $29.53), now $15 for 2 sets.
  • Burger King From now until 30 April, enjoy 2x Mushroom Swiss, 1x Tendergrill Chicken and 2x Medium Fries for only $14.90.
  • Kimly Seafood From now until 15 April, enjoy 30% off set meals.

CELEBRATE EARTH MONTH WITH IMPOSSIBLE FOODS, AVAILABLE ON DELIVEROO

Save the Earth, even if you're staying home. Available to enjoy at home via Deliveroo, Impossible Foods, the makers of delicious meat made from plants, is celebrating Earth Month with a new campaign that aims to showcase the collective impact of choosing plant-based meat, and how individual food choices can reduce Singapore's total environmental impact.

Special Earth Month dishes available via Deliveroo include a new plant-based menu from Three Buns, featuring new burgers like the Impossible Hombre, a Mexican-inspired stack with an Impossible meat patty, hot chilli and roasted peppers; and Le Big Mock, a plant-based take on the famous burger with smashed Impossible meat patties, dill and toasted caraway seed and vegan cheddar on a toasted mango flour sesame bun, while PizzaExpress will introduce the Impossible Teriyaki Pizza, and the fusion Impossible Teriyaki Spaghetti. PS Cafe will launch the all-new Impossible Thai Brunch Bowl and Impossible Bolognese Pizza, and NamNam Noodle Bar will be serving up Vietnamese favourites Banh Mi Impossible, Dry Braised Impossible and Pho Impossible.

Throughout April, customers who order Impossible dishes from participating restaurants will be rewarded with special limited edition Earth Month merchandise. At the end of the campaign, Impossible Foods will unveil the total sustainability impact across all of the participating restaurants, driven entirely by the planet-conscious choices made by customers.
