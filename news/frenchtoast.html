Deliveroo Raises a Toast to National French Toast Day

  • 3 December 2018
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 3 min
foodtrends frenchtoast
Share this article
  • Because food makes everything 'butter'

Psst psst! Want to know the secret to making hump day a little bit jammier? With National French Toast Day happening this Wednesday, 28 November, there's no better reason to forget white bread and tuck into a scrumptious breakfast treat instead. Whether you prefer your toast classic or creative, Deliveroo, Singapore's most loved food delivery service, has curated a delightfully decadent list of French Toasts to suit all tastes.

So where can you find the most mouthwatering French Toast dishes across the foodie paradise that is the Little Red Dot?

French Toast – Killiney Kopitiam ($2.89)

Both true-blue Singaporeans and curious tourists can enjoy a local breakfast experience with Killiney Kopitiam's French Toast with kaya and butter. Butter up for this simple but delicious option!

French Toast with Berries & Mascarpone – One Man Coffee ($12.00)

Topped with tart berries and creamy mascarpone, One Man Coffee's picture-perfect French Toast makes for that oh-so-Instagrammable shot. Influencer or not, feeding both your Instagram feed and your tummies are a must.

Sweet Morning – Wild Honey ($20.00)

Treat yourself by indulging in Wild Honey's twice cooked signature brioche French Toast. Stuffed with grilled mango, vanilla bean mascarpone and hibiscus-berry compote, this chef-recommended dish is sure to put some yummy in your tummy. 

French Toast – PAUL ($8.90)

Next up is PAUL's toasted brioche French delight. Lightly dusted with powdered sugar and served with vanilla cream cheese, jam and honey, this sweet treat will certainly satisfy sweet-toothed diners.

Peanut French Toast – The Lazy Garden ($4.90)

For all you broke students out there, look no further! A bountiful bargain, The Lazy Garden's offering of bread fried in egg served with peanut butter, is sure to fill you right up without burning that hole in your wallet.                                     

Brioche French Toast – Clinton Street Baking Co. & Restaurant ($19.26)

Stick to the origins of French Toast with Clinton's Brioche dish. Crafted for a true taste sensation, light as air brioche is decorated topped with caramelized bananas, roasted pecans, and a drizzle of warm maple butter. Bon Appétit!

Vegetarian French Toast – The Lokal ($18.19)

Vegetarians, do not fret! The Lokal's offering toast with banana, passionfruit curd, coconut and gula melaka-pandan syrup will ensure you get your French Toast fix. What could be sweeter?

Brulee French Toast – Group Therapy* ($18.00)

For a fluffy and eggy party in your mouth, dig in to this warm buttery French toast that is paired with berry compote, almonds, grapefruit and cold salted caramel & vanilla ice cream. An explosion of flavours and textures!

The best thing since sliced bread, literally: What could be better than flavoursome French Toast delivered straight to your door for breakfast, lunch, or even dinner.? No judgement here – treat yourselves this French Toast Day!

*Group Therapy's Brulee French Toast is available on weekends only
