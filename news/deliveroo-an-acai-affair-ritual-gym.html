Overindulged during the festive season? Deliveroo teams up with An Açaí Affair and Ritual Gym to help get your fitness back on track

  • 15 February 2022
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 4 min
deliveroo an açaí affair ritual gym healthy bundle
Share this article
  • From 1 to 14 March, Deliveroo customers can get their hands on An Açaí Affair's Nutty Party bundle
  • 30 lucky winners stand a chance to win a 14-day unlimited starter pack from Ritual Gym

Chinese New Year has come and gone, officially marking the end of the festive season. With plenty of scrumptious meals and sweet treats offered during this period, it is easy to fall off the fitness wagon. To help customers get a roaring start to the Year of the Tiger, Deliveroo has teamed up with Singapore's largest organic açaí specialty cafe chain, An Açaí Affair, and homegrown boutique fitness brand, Ritual Gym to curate a limited-edition Nutty Party Bundle.

Each Nutty Party Bundle (S$35) includes three Nutty Party bowls from An Açaí Affair. This signature bowl features açaí with banana, granola, nuts and cacao nibs, perfect as a guilt-free snack or an after-workout refresher. That's not all - 30 lucky Deliveroo customers who cop this bundle will win a 14-day unlimited starter pack* from Ritual Gym.

Perfect for contemporary time-strapped Singaporeans, Ritual Gym offers quick 20-min sessions that still pack a punch. Each session offers a lung-busting combination of strength training and cardio that will build muscle, burn fat and keep you coming back for more. Thanks to the afterburn effect, the 20-minute session can raise your metabolic rate and help you burn fat for up to 48 hours! The best part? Customers can access all five Ritual Gym locations** across the island with the starter pack.

From 1 to 14 March 2022, customers can get their hands on the limited-edition bundle from all An Açaí Affair outlets*** on Deliveroo at $35. Only 30 lucky customers will be able to receive the limited-edition bundles with free Ritual 14-day passes. Don't miss out!
