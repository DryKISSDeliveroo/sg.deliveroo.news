Kick-start a sustainable diet this 2022 with Deliveroo and Burger King! Back by popular demand, the Plant-Based WHOPPER®, is here to stay.

  • 12 January 2022
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 3 min
deliveroo burger king plant-based whopper
Share this article
  • With 100% real ingredients, no colours, flavours or preservatives from artificial sources, you can now enjoy Burger King's plant-based burger with a peace of mind!

If your New Year resolution is to improve your diet, you are not alone! 2022 rings in a fresh start and an opportunity to make positive changes in our lives. Maintaining a healthier and sustainable diet is definitely on the to-do list for most of us, as evident from the plant-based phenomenon that continues to sweep the nation. In fact, Deliveroo's latest Top 100 Report shows that  clean and sustainable eats, including customisable salad bowls, continue to rank among the top ordered dishes last year.  

With this in mind, Singapore's favourite food delivery service, Deliveroo, is committed to helping foodies kick-start the new year with sustainable and healthier choices in the way that they eat. Available from 14 January, customers will be the first to get their hands on the new and improved Plant-based WHOPPER® for home delivery via Deliveroo!

With the same flame-grilled WHOPPER® taste, the all-improved Plant-based WHOPPER® features 100% real ingredients and contains zero colours, flavours or preservatives from artificial sources, allowing eco-conscious foodies to leave a green footprint, one meal at a time.

This improved version of the Plant-based WHOPPER® will be available as ala-carte at S$9.50 or as part of Deliveroo's exclusive $15 bundle (comes with 6pc BK Nuggets, medium onion rings, taro pie and a small Coke Zero) and the Plant-based WHOPPER® Burger Meal (comes with a medium Coke Zero and large fries) at S$11.90 and, exclusively for home delivery from participating Burger King restaurants* via Deliveroo from 14 January. That's not all - the first 500 Deliveroo customers to order the Plant-based WHOPPER® Burger Meal can enjoy a 1-for-1 deal only on 14 January, so don't miss out!

As a socially responsible company, Deliveroo is committed to empowering foodies to make more environmentally friendly choices. The exclusive Plant-based WHOPPER® is the first of many exciting initiatives that customers can look forward to in 2022. Eating healthier and sustainably  has never been easier (and tastier) than with Deliveroo!
