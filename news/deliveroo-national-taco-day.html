Snag a taco-in-a-taco limited-edition kit this National Taco Day from Deliveroo and Guzman Y Gomez

  • 28 September 2021
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 2 min
deliveroo national taco day guzman y gomez
Share this article
  • Let's Taco 'Bout Your Love for Tacos

Taco lovers, get ready to feast! In celebration of National Taco Day on 4 October, Singapore's most loved food delivery service, Deliveroo, has partnered with popular Mexican restaurant Guzman Y Gomez to create limited-edition taco kits - guaranteed to make a die-hard taco fan swoon.

Exclusively available on National Taco Day, each order of three regular hard or soft shell tacos from Guzman Y Gomez via Deliveroo will come with a taco-shaped carrier that allows you to tote around your box of tacos with pride. A smorgasbord of colours awaits as you open the kit to reveal illustrations of all the quintessential taco ingredients you can think of, from brightly coloured tomatoes, cucumber to cheese. That's not all. Customers who manage to snag the limited-edition kit will be rewarded with $20 worth of Deliveroo credits! Because nothing says 'I love tacos' like a taco-shaped kit full of yummy tacos.

Whether you are in the mood for soft or hard shell tacos, each taco is served with Guzman Y Gomez's house-blend salsa, pico de gallo (freshly chopped salsa), Australian Jack cheese and a juicy lime wedge. Customers can choose from an array of tantalising meat fillings such as grilled chicken, grilled steak, pulled pork and pan-seared barramundi. You can even opt to go vegan with the sauteed veggie with guacamole option.

The limited-edition taco kits will be available for delivery on Deliveroo on 4 October 2021, exclusively at Guzman Y Gomez's Tanjong Pagar Centre and Funan Mall outlets, for $13, while stocks last. There are only a limited quantity of kits up for grabs, so call your fellow taco lover and set your alarms to place your orders! To sweeten the deal, the first 15 customers to order the limited-edition taco kit will receive free churros, the perfect dessert to end your Mexican feast.
