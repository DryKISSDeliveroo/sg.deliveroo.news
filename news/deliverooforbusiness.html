Order in a Barista with Deliveroo for Business this International Coffee Day

  • 23 September 2019
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 3 min
deliveroo for business
Share this article
  • Win a caffeine fix delivered straight to your office to fuel you and your colleagues

Whether it be a morning ritual, an afternoon break paired with a sweet treat, or a catch-up with colleagues and friends, coffee is an undeniable part of everyday life in Singapore. This International Coffee Day, 1 October, Deliveroo for Business, the dedicated service for simple and convenient office catering and corporate food delivery by Deliveroo, ups the ante by offering office workers the chance to order in a barista to amp up their work day.

In partnership with Musketeers Coffee, Deliveroo for Business will deliver a barista directly to three offices on International Coffee Day, where they'll serve up fresh, piping hot brews for an hour each. From 23 September to 25 September, coffee fanatics can nominate their office on Facebook by spilling the beans on their caffeine obsession. To win a barista for the whole office on International Coffee Day, coffee fans simply have to share why their office is most deserving of a free caffeine fix in 50 words or less.* Creative submissions are encouraged and no secrets will be roasted in the process.

The three best entries will receive a well-deserved visit from a personal barista on International Coffee Day, where the entire office will enjoy an hour of freshly brewed Long Black or Cafe Latte from Musketeers Coffee. So to avoid depresso when there's no expresso, get your submissions in today - and bag yourself a few new office BFFs too!

Beyond International Coffee Day, companies can also sign up for Deliveroo for Business, and treat their team and clients to the tastiest restaurant food, on demand. From client meetings and office parties, to team lunches and late night workers, Deliveroo for Business is reimagining food in the workplace, and making teams happier and more efficient, one office at a time.

With Deliveroo for Business:

● Employees can order from thousands of restaurants and pay directly via a company allowance. Say goodbye to expense reports and fiddly receipts.

● Catering for team meetings and events is a breeze with dedicated account managers to help get everything sorted.

● You control your company spend and cut costs with customizable ordering rules, project codes, and monthly invoices.

Find out more about Deliveroo for Business here: https://deliveroo.com.sg/business/about
