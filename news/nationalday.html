Deliveroo Exclusive: Burger King Says “Happy Birthday Singapore”

  • 15 July 2019
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 4 min
national day
Share this article
  • Available for one day only, the special National Day combo for two is a 'die die must try'

To celebrate the nation's 54th birthday, Deliveroo has joined forces with Burger King to surprise foodies in Singapore with a special Super Shiok Set. The combo meal for two features local flavours that are universally loved, so you can stay in and watch the National Day Parade with a suitably Singaporean feast.

Available exclusively on Deliveroo on 9 August, the Super Shiok Set will serve up Burger King's Taste of Singapore burgers for hungry foodies to order in. Burger King's regular comeback flavour every National Day, the Rendang Burger features aromatic Rendang sauce and crunchy fresh onions layered on a single flame grilled Tendergrill® chicken patty (thigh meat) or two flame-grilled, 100% beef patties served on a toasted sesame seed bun. The fan favourite will be accompanied by a new local flavour - the Laksa Burger - this year, which comes with slices of hard-boiled egg, Laksa sauce made with real ingredients like dried shrimp, laksa leaves and spices on two flame-grilled, 100% beef patties or a single Tendercrisp® chicken patty (coated thigh meat) between a sesame seed bun. For a refreshing and crunchy mouthfeel, cucumbers will also star in Burger King's version of the Laksa Burger.

Each limited edition set is priced at $26, and will include your choice of two Taste of Singapore burgers, one piece fried chicken, one large fries, two Hershey's Sundae Pie and two cans of Authentic Tea House - Yin Hao. And since it's National Day, the Super Shiok Set will also serve up an extra dose of love for our Little Red Dot. Every order will come in a collectible box along with a tea towel, both featuring an artistic mashup of Singapore, Deliveroo and Burger King - how 'swee' is that?

The Super Shiok Set will be available exclusively on Deliveroo from 13 Burger King restaurants across the island: The Central, Cineleisure, JEM, Jurong Point, Kebun Baru Community Club, NEX, Parkway Parade, Pasir Ris Sports Centre, Seletar Mall, Tampines North Community Club, Toa Payoh, VIVA Business Park, and Yishun Town Square. But you'll have to be quick when pre-orders start on 8 August, because there's only 130 of these limited edition sets up for grabs.*

Fancy even more local delights? Deliveroo will also be serving up various promotions including weekly flash deals, all in celebration of Singapore's 54th birthday! From 22 July to 11 August, simply look out for the "Local Gems" tag on the Deliveroo app to see the full list of restaurants on offer. For more details on the promotions, refer to Annex A.

*While stocks last at all participating outlets
