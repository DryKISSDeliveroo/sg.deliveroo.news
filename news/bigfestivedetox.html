Burning Off Those Festive Calories At the Gym? Take Your Big Festive Detox Up A Notch with Deliveroo

  • 10 February 2020
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 5 min
big festive detox
Share this article
  • Make that workout work harder with delectable and healthy combos from Deliveroo, delivered direct to your gym doorstep

A bevy of festive activities have come and gone, bringing with it the new year resolutions we've set just a month ago. If you're feeling like you're at ground zero, fret not as Singapore's most-loved food delivery service Deliveroo, has partnered four of Singapore's favourite restaurant partners to curate healthy combo meals to help you refuel the right way after your workouts, and kickstart that post-festive detox!

From 10 to 16 February 2020, SaladStop!, Heybo, Harvestry and Shake Farm will be offering food and drink combo meals, exclusively on Deliveroo, to help replenish the nutrients you've lost after working your guts out at the gym. Worked your core during barre and yoga? Try out the Lean and Green combo. Hit a new weightlifting goal at the gym? Replenish your protein with Protein Train. And for those who burned hundreds of calories at your HIIT class, be sure to opt for the Electrolyte Fuel.

Lean and Green

Ideal for light to medium activities with an emphasis on flexibility and toning, like barre, yoga and pilates. Lean and Green provides the right amount of electrolytes and protein from delicious grain and salad bowls, coupled with a thirst-quenching beverage to reward yourself after your workout!

SaladStop!* Iron "Wo" Man and Coconut Water ($12.70)

A medley of baby spinach, feta, roasted pumpkin, raisins, toasted almonds, raspberry vinaigrette

Heybo (Raffles Place) Made Possible and Sparkling Soda ($15.20)

Garden greens, mixed grains, Impossible™ Patties, roasted pumpkin, roasted brussel sprouts, tomato salad, seed mix with red pepper dip & green pesto. Dietary: vegan, contains gluten.

Harvestry (Tanjong Pagar) Soba So Good and Cold-Pressed Juice ($12.75)

Soba noodles with sesame dressing, pumpkins, tomatoes, edamame, pumpkin seeds and purple coleslaw

Harvestry (Tanjong Pagar) Skinny Down and Cold-Pressed Juice ($15.30)

Chicken breast, spinach, tomatoes, cucumber pickle, corn salad, furikake, basil yoghurt on brown rice

Shake Farm (Telok Ayer) Green Goodness and Clean Cleanse smoothie ($20.80)

Greek feta cheese, smashed avocado, homemade tahini hummus, cherry tomatoes, green olives, cucumber, cashew nuts, mixed seeds, homemade balsamic dressing

Clean cleanse is a blended smoothie of kiwi,green apple, spinach, celery, chia seeds, acai berry pulp and almond milk

Protein Train

Lifting heavy weights during activities like Crossfit, weightlifting and TRX can add tremendous strain to your muscles, which is why nutritionists recommend consuming an ample amount of protein and unrefined carbohydrates to repair and rebuild muscle fibers post-workout. Enjoy a protein-loaded meal and guzzle down a refreshing drink after pushing the bar during your workout.

SaladStop!* Ou La Lah and coconut water ($14.20)

Baby spinach, rocket, sustainable smoked salmon, feta, roasted brussel sprout, french beans, cherry tomatoes, seed mix, balsamic vinaigrette, honey dijon

Heybo (Raffles Place) Muscle Beach and your choice of sparkling soda ($11.90)

Tricoloured quinoa, sous-vide chicken breast, charred broccoli, charred carrots, avocado, seed mix with Beetroot Chipotle Dop and Yuzu Soy

Harvestry (Tanjong Pagar) Team Protein and your choice of cold-pressed juice ($17)

Chicken breast served with onsen egg, carrot, broccoli, spinach with lemon dressing on mixed grains

Shake Farm (Telok Ayer) Grandma's Roast and Clean Cut Smoothie ($23.92)

Roast Jerk Chicken, sweet potato and carrot mash, roasted garden vegetables, garlic broccoli, dried cranberries, farmhouse gravy and parsley

Clean cut consists of a blend of avocado, pineapple, kale, spinach, chia seeds, vanilla whey protein and coconut water

Electrolyte Fuel

Fast-paced and endurance-based activities like spinning, boxing and HIIT (high intensity interval training) requires a surplus of electrolytes, complex carbs and dietary fiber to repair the rapid muscle breakdown process and dehydration from the extreme cardio and intensity.

SaladStop!* Jai Ho! and coconut water ($12.70)

Romaine lettuce, tandoori chicken, potato, warm spiced chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, capsicum, cucumber, indian spiced yoghurt

Heybo (Raffles Place) Shibuya Nights and choice of sparkling soda ($14.20)

Green soba, baked salmon, onsen egg, grilled mushrooms, oriental cabbage salad, furikake with Avocado Edamame Dip & Beetroot Miso

Harvestry (Tanjong Pagar) Clean and Lean and choice of cold-pressed juice ($14)

Baked salmon fillet with a serving of broccoli, mushrooms, tomatoes on romaine lettuce with lemon zest dressing

Shake Farm (Telok Ayer) Perfect Pesto and coconut water ($17.52)

Homemade basil pesto, Greek feta cheese, cherry tomatoes, red onions, toasted almonds, dried cranberries, homemade balsamic dressing and mixed leaves

There's no time to waste, kick start your big festive detox now! To pre-order in after your sweat sesh, simply select a time, input your gym's postal code, get a good workout in and wait for your fuel to arrive straight to your gym.

* Available at the following SaladStop! Branches: Capital Tower, The Cathay,  Changi UE BizHub East, DUO Galleria, Fusionopolis (Portsdown), Great World City, Harbourfront Tower 1, Metropolis, Novena Square, One George Street, Republic Plaza, SAIL, Tanglin Mall and Tangs
