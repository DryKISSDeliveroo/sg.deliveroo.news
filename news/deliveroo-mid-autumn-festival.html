Deliveroo and Swensen’s Delivers Mid-Autumn Magic To Singaporeans With Festive Mooncake and Lantern Set

  • 7 September 2020
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 2 min
mid-autumn festival swensen's
Share this article
  • Light up your evening with 20 limited-edition Deliveroo lanterns, up for grabs

Lit lanterns, a full moon and delicious mooncakes - the season of thanksgiving and good fortune is upon us once again! In celebration of this year's Mid-Autumn Festival, Singapore's favourite food delivery service Deliveroo has teamed up with local family favourite Swensen's to bring mid-autumn magic into the home with an exclusive mooncake and lantern bundle, perfect for an illuminating night in.

Created especially for the occasion, customers can curate their own mooncake box and pick any four of Swensen's newest mochi-snowskin ice cream mooncakes. Popular flavours include Sticky Chewy Chocolate, Durian King, Lotus Paste and Grapefruit. Each Swensen's Create Your Own Mooncake Box also comes with a special limited-edition Deliveroo paper lantern. Designed by a local papercraft artist, the lantern comes beautifully adorned with ornate paper flowers in Deliveroo's signature teal and white colours and is further embellished with a handful of meticulously crafted paper butterflies littered across the lantern. Each lantern also features a striking paper cut-out of the iconic Deliveroo roo head and comes with a LED light that shows the silhouette downcast when lit, providing the perfect addition to your evening activities or even as a decorative piece in your home!

To get your hands on the limited-edition Deliveroo lantern, simply order the Swensen's Create Your Own Mooncake Box set ($39.80) from Swensen's Thomson Plaza from 21 to 27 September. There are only 20 paper lanterns up for grabs, so mark your calendar and get ready to reminisce timeless memories under the warm lantern glow in the comfort of your home.

*Limited quantities available, whilst stocks last.
