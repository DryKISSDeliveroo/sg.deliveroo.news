Deliveroo Celebrates Singles’ Day with Delicious Deals

  • 5 November 2018
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 2 min
singlesday diningsolo solo single
Share this article
  • Dining solo has never been this delightful

This November 11, get ready to napkin up and enjoy the perfect spread for one. To celebrate singledom and the joys that come with flying solo (including not having to share your food), Singapore's most loved food delivery service Deliveroo is offering an ambrosial array of fan favourites at an absolute steal. For one day only,* just $1 will snag you succulent 3-piece Tenders from New Orleans-style fried chicken chain Popeyes Louisiana Kitchen. Or if the hot sun's got you hiding out indoors during lunch time, just order in Tuk Tuk Cha's Cold Thai Milk Tea, also available at just $1, to beat the heat! Should you be in the mood for something a little fancier, order up some scrumptious Salmon Don and Oolong Tea from Sushi Tei at $11. Dining solo can't get any better than this, or can it? Because there's no reason why you should only enjoy a little self-lovin' on Singles' Day, we've teamed up with a range of restaurants to create more delectable deals** available via Deliveroo from 1 to 11 November 2018. Tuck into a hearty bowl of Habanero Chicken Poké ($11) from Aloha Poké that packs just the right amount of heat, you'll be reaching for your phone for a second order in no time. Or for a 'finger-licking-good' treat, get stuck into Sarpino's 8 piece wings set ($1), 4 mid joints and 4 drumlets. From sweet to savoury, meals to munchies, there is surely something for you this Single's Day. From now till Singles' Day, we're also rewarding solo diners who embrace eating alone! Simply upload a photo of your me-time meal on Facebook or Instagram, and tag Deliveroo (@DeliverooSG / @deliveroo_sg) with a hashtag #metimemeal in the post for a chance to win $100 worth of Deliveroo credits. One lucky foodie will be selected between 1-11 Nov, to enjoy more great food. Don't also forget that Deliveroo has recently dropped minimum spend to $0,*** (yes you heard it right!), across various heartland regions in Singapore. So that's one more reason to get your solo dining on this Single's Day! They say that all you need is love and at Deliveroo, we'd like to think food is love. So, go on and order up that feast you know you deserve. Filter by 'Singles Day Deals' to search for participating restaurants.
