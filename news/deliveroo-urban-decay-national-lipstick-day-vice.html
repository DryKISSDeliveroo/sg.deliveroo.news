Deliveroo Encourages Foodies to Put Urban Decay’s New Vice Lipstick to The Ultimate Test this National Lipstick Day with Editions Giveaway

  • 21 July 2021
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 3 min
national lipstick day urban decay vice
Share this article
  • From 27-29 July, Deliveroo customers to get exclusive dibs on Urban Decay's newest lippies one week ahead of official launch

We all want our lipsticks to last long, but some just aren't up to the task. This National Lipstick Day, 29 July, Deliveroo exclusively partners American cult beauty favourite Urban Decay to put their new Vice lipstick to the ultimate test, best trialled over some lip-smackingly tasty food from Editions.

Inspired by the grit and glamour of Los Angeles, Vice is the only 100% vegan and 100% high impact lipstick that lasts all day, thanks to extreme pigmentation and hydration infused with avocado oil and aloe vera. From 27-29 July, Deliveroo customers will be the first to snag these exclusive lipsticks for themselves with orders from participating Deliveroo Editions restaurants.

From Omakase Burger's ultra juicy burgers and crispy fries to Mr Rooster's freshly prepared chicken wings, foodies are spoilt for choice when it comes to chowing down in style (literally). Thanks to Vice's unique formula, you can now feast in peace knowing there'll be no bleeding or feathering this National Lipstick Day and beyond!

Available in 34 bold, bright and nude shades, Vice's superior vegan formula comes in three unique finishes: Matte, Cream and Shine, while the sleek A-shell gunmetal case with imperfect dents is uniquely designed to match each finish family. Priced at $33 RSP, Vice lipsticks will be available to purchase online at Sephora.sg from 3 August and all Sephora stores from Thursday, 5 August.

From 27-29 July 2021, every order from participating restaurants* via the Deliveroo Editions sites at Katong and Alice@Mediapolis allows you to opt in for a free Urban Decay Vice lipstick, available via in-app delivery orders or pick up, whilst stocks last. Now all you need to do is perfect that pout!
