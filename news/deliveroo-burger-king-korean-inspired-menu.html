Daebak! Deliveroo foodies among first to try BURGER KING®’s new Korean-inspired menu

  • 15 September 2021
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 4 min
deliveroo burger king korean
Share this article
  • A flavourful limited series coming to your Deliveroo app screens this fall

If you're a fan of Korean entertainment and culture, then why not transport yourself to this iconic destination by way of your taste buds? Deliveroo and BURGER KING® have teamed up to satisfy your wanderlust with a new Korean-inspired menu, exclusively available on Deliveroo* for home delivery from 17 September. That means that food-obsessed Deliveroo fans will get the first look (and taste!) of the mouth-watering line-up before it makes its debut in BURGER KING® stores from 21 September. It's time to meet the cast of this release, who complement each other like a satisfying meal, literally!

Because Koreans love all things fried chicken, it's only right to treat yourself to the new Ultimate Cheesy Dynamite Tendercrisp Chicken ($11.50) and Cheesy Dynamite Nuggets (nine pieces for $7.10) for a power duo like no other, and complete your meal with Korean-inspired sides - the Cheesy Dynamite Fries ($3.80) and Dynamite Shake ($5.20). Ordering for many? Feast on Deliveroo's exclusive $15 Bundle which includes an Ultimate Cheesy Dynamite Tendercrisp, six-piece nuggets with BBQ sauce, a small coke and a New York Cheesecake Pie!

The Ultimate Cheesy Dynamite Tendercrisp Chicken features BURGER KING®'s proprietary fried spicy chicken patty, paired to perfection with a Korean ramyeon-inspired sauce for a fiery kick. Nacho cheese and American cheese lend creaminess to the burger in the same way that it would for Korean instant noodles, and give this premium dish a dynamic yet balanced flavour.

Nugget fans can rejoice with the news of their favourite item now being the star of a meal! The Cheesy Dynamite Nuggets signifies the first time BURGER KING® is creating a special Nuggets Meal with a special accompanying sauce. Fans can choose to dip these delicious treats from either the Nacho Cheese or Spicy Korean sauces.

Cool down between bites with the Cheesy Dynamite Fries, an extension of BURGER KING®'s classic cheese fries with a Korean-inspired twist! To shake things up (literally!), try the all-new Dynamite Shake which allows you to shake and evenly coat the Mexican Drumlets with Korean ramyeon-inspired sauce, much like you would if you were at a local Korean fried chicken outlet.

What's more, consumers can participate in a weekly draw where winners will stand a chance to win an iPhone 12, $120 worth of Deliveroo vouchers or $100 BURGER KING® vouchers when they purchase any Dynamite Korean items. As a bonus, Deliveroo customers get three additional chances to win! Simply SMS BKSG<space>Name<space>Receipt Number to 9010 2322.

If you're now thinking bae-go-pah ("I'm hungry"), you're not the only one! Mark your calendars for the anticipated premiere on Deliveroo.
