This National Day, stay-home celebrations don't have to be bland or boring thanks to pocket-friendly promotions on your favourite local delights available on Deliveroo. Singapore's most-loved food delivery service has compiled a selection of true blue Singaporean dishes to order-in for patriotic feasting with family and friends.

Perfect for those looking to celebrate the NDP festivities from the safety of their sofa, there's a range of mouthwatering makan available to suit all tastes. Better still, with various bundle deals and promotions, these dishes ranging from succulent chicken rice, to spicy laksa, to fragrant nasi lemak will satisfy your tastebuds without breaking the bank. Believe us when we say these are die die must try!
