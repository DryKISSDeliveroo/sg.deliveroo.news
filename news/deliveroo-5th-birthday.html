Deliveroo celebrates 5th birthday with a limited-edition birthday cake and over $150,000 worth of prizes

  • 27 October 2020
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 3 min
deliveroo anniversary 5th birthday staycation
Share this article
  • Stand a chance to win dining vouchers or a swanky two-day family staycation package during Deliveroo's birthday month!  

Singapore's most loved food delivery service, Deliveroo, is ushering in its 5th birthday in Singapore! To mark this milestone, Deliveroo has created the ultimate birthday cake that pays tribute to the local flavours of the Little Red Dot.

Decorated with strokes of teal and crowned with the unmistakable Deliveroo logo, the four-inch  Deliveroo birthday cake serves up to five people and packs five yummy local favourites in one bite: brown sugar mochi, coconut gula melaka, pandan, red bean and kopi vanilla. Delicate macarons in Deliveroo's signature teal also adorn the cake to satisfy sweet-toothed foodies. The combination of sweet and earthy flavours make for a satisfying slice that is worth all the calories, with no questions asked if you go for seconds. Each cake also comes with birthday candles and festive toppers to complete the celebratory theme.

From 5 to 9 November, customers can purchase these one-of-a-kind Deliveroo birthday cakes for their own celebrations with loved ones, for just $15 under the Mix & Match menu of all three Deliveroo Editions sites at Katong, Alice@Mediapolis and CT Hub 2. The cakes will be available for both dine-in and delivery orders, whilst stocks last.

If you're craving for a well-deserved break to go with your slice of cake, Deliveroo has also organised daily giveaways for its birthday month, with over $150,000 in total prize value up for grabs. From 2 to 29 November, 28 lucky foodies can stand a chance to win $5 or $10 dining vouchers or the grand prize of a luxurious 2D1N family staycation at The Fullerton Hotel or The Fullerton Bay Hotel. Joining the giveaway is easy! Simply place an order over $30 on Deliveroo from any restaurant from 2 to 29 November and you will be in the running.  One lucky winner will be selected at random each day and revealed on Deliveroo's Facebook page.

If you're a foodie, you're invited! Don't miss Deliveroo's birthday celebrations this November.

Note to Editors:

●        The winners of the staycation packages will be announced on Deliveroo's Facebook page twice a week. Each package includes breakfast for two adults and two kids below 11 years old, complimentary bed for kids below 11 years old and dining credits.

●        The winners of the vouchers will receive instant vouchers in their registered email address with Deliveroo.
