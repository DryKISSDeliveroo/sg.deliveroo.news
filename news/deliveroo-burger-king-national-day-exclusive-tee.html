Profess Your Love for the Iconic Rendang Beef Burger With Deliveroo and BURGER KING®’s Exclusive National Day Tee

  • 13 July 2021
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 4 min
national day burger king exclusive tee
Share this article
  • Brings a whole new meaning to wearing your heart on your sleeve!

Been chowing down on BURGER KING®'s new Double Ultimate Rendang Angus Beef Burger since it exclusively launched on Deliveroo last week? At this most patriotic time of the year, you can now profess your love for the iconic Rendang Burger loud and proud with the launch of limited-edition Deliveroo x BURGER KING® National Day tees.

With a design inspired by Deliveroo's iconic teal app and BURGER KING®'s much loved Rendang Burger, the limited-edition tee is a tongue-in-cheek ode to both iconic brands this National Day. What better way to profess your love for food and country than with a National Day-themed Deliveroo order held close to your heart (and printed on soft cotton). The simple yet witty white tee was designed for foodies by foodies, and features playful acronyms that Rendang fanatics will recognise. Can you spot local acronyms like Sourdough Bun Stack (SBS), Carefully, Perfectly, Flame-grilled (CPF), Ultimate Rendang Angus (URA) and Home Delivered Burger (HDB)? Show your pride for a Singaporean favourite and be the envy of fellow Rendang lovers when sporting this look!

From 30 July, get your hands on this limited-edition collectible by purchasing the Taste of Singapore Indulgence Bundle, available exclusively on Deliveroo at participating Burger King outlets* for a pocket-friendly price of $15. The bundle features a Double Rendang Beef, box of four-piece Nuggets, Hershey Sundae Pie and Sjora Mango Peach. There are only 85 tees up for grabs, so fastest fingers first!

Celebrate our Nation's birthday in style with the ultimate at-home fast food feast with all-time shiok favourites from BURGER KING® restaurants** via Deliveroo. The classic Double Rendang Beef and Rendang Tendergrill® Chicken Burger feature either a double flame-grilled beef patty, or juicy Tendergrill® chicken thigh patty, both topped with signature, aromatic Rendang sauce and fresh onions, served on toasted sesame seed buns. For a levelled-up experience, try the new Double Ultimate Rendang Angus Beef Burger, which features two juicy, tender Angus flame-grilled beef patties, topped with BURGER KING®'s signature, sedap Rendang sauce and fresh onions, complete  strips of crispy bacon between fluffy corn-dusted sourdough buns. Sedap!

There's a Rendang for everyone this National Day, so kick back and relax with your family and friends in your new garms, and don't forget your napkins!

*From 30 July, 85 limited-edition Deliveroo x BURGER KING® National Day tees will be exclusively available via delivery only from  BURGER KING®'s Bukit Panjang Plaza, Jurong Point, Seletar Mall, Tampines North Community Club, Yishun and Viva Business Park outlets, while stocks last.
