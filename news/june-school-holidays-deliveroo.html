Famished after a day of fun? Deliveroo Singapore delivers straight to playgrounds this June school holidays

  • 23 May 2022
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 4 min
deliveroo june school holidays playground food delivery
Share this article

Pick from a selection of family-friendly restaurants to recharge your kiddos after playtime.

The June school holidays are fast approaching, and children's laughter will soon return to playgrounds as they trade homework for days of endless fun. Following popular demand from parents and in anticipation of the little ones getting famished after a day of play, Deliveroo Singapore is announcing that you can get mouth-watering meals from your favourite restaurants delivered directly to playgrounds islandwide.

From light bites such as chicken wings and truffle fries to hearty meals like burgers and pasta, parents can easily order their kids' favourite meals on Deliveroo in four easy steps:

  • Download or open the Deliveroo app for iOS or Android
  • Enter your location
  • Drop the delivery pin at the playground
  • Order your meal for yourself and the little ones. Once your order is nearby, you will receive a delivery notification. Prepare to meet your rider!

Say goodbye to cumbersome picnic baskets and soggy sandwiches, and hello to a variety of food options at your fingertips! Need some food inspiration? Deliveroo has curated a list of kid meals and family bundles that are sure to make the kiddos squeal with delight.
